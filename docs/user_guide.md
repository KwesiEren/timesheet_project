# User Guide

Welcome to the Timesheet Application! this guide will walk you through the essential features and how to manage your daily work hours effectively.

## 1. Getting Started

### Registration
- Open the app and tap on **Register**.
- Fill in your **Name**, **Email**, and **Password**.
- You can optionally upload a profile picture.
- Tap **Sign Up** to create your account.

### Logging In
- Enter your email and password on the login screen.
- Toggle **Remember Me** if you want the app to stay logged in on this device.
- Tap **Login** to access your dashboard.

## 2. Daily Attendance (Check-In/Out)

The app helps you log your total "On Duty" time each day.

- **Check-In**: Tap the **Arrival** button on the dashboard when you start your workday.
- **Check-Out**: Tap the **Departure** button when you finish.
- The dashboard will show your total elapsed time for the current day.

## 3. Managing Activities (Tasks)

You can track specific tasks (Activities) throughout your day to provide a detailed breakdown of your work.

- **Start an Activity**: Tap the **+** icon or the **Activities** button. Enter the title and details. Tap **Start Timer**.
- **Timer**: You can pause and resume activities if you take a break.
- **Complete Activity**: When finished, tap **Stop/Complete**. This will save the final duration to your timesheet.
- **View History**: All activities are saved and can be viewed in the **Activities** page, grouped by date.

## 4. Notifications & Announcements

Stay up to date with important news and alerts.

- **Announcements**: Check the **Dashboard** or the **Announcements** page for company-wide updates.
- **Notifications**: Tap the bell icon to see personal alerts, such as reminders to check-out or approval status for your activities.

## 5. Dashboard & Calendar

- **Dashboard**: Your go-to place for current status, active tasks, and quick summaries.
- **Calendar**: Use the **Calendar** tab to navigate back to previous dates and see historical attendance and activity data.
- **Charts**: Visual charts on the dashboard help you see your work distribution over the past week.

## 💡 Pro Tips
- **Check your Dashboard** every morning to ensure you've checked in.
- **Add Notes** to your activities to make it easier for managers to understand what was accomplished.
- **Keep the app running** in the background while your activity timer is active; it will continue to track your time.
